Returns Policy

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing Grand Occasion Rental for your event equipment rental needs. This Returns Policy explains our policies regarding returns, cancellations, and refunds for equipment rented through our website.

2. Rental Cancellation

2.1. Cancellation Period

You have the right to cancel your equipment rental order within 24hours prior to booked/agreed delivery date. Please note that the specific cancellation period may vary depending on the type of equipment rented.

2.2. How to Cancel

To cancel a rental, you must contact us by sending us an email or calling us via info@grandoccasionrental.ie/ 0851563498 and provide your booking details. We will guide you through the cancellation process and any associated fees.

3. Refunds

3.1. Full Refunds

If you cancel within 48hours or more prior to booked/agreed delivery date, you are entitled to a full refund of any fees paid for the equipment rental. (Terms and Condition Applies) Refunds will be processed within seven (7) days after your cancellation request.

3.2. Partial Refunds

If you cancel within 48hours to your booked/agreed delivery date, you may be eligible for a partial refund (Terms and Condition Applies). The amount of the refund will depend on the time remaining until the scheduled rental date and the equipment's availability.

4. Damaged or Defective Equipment
If the equipment you receive is damaged or defective, please notify us immediately. We will work to replace the equipment or provide a refund based on the circumstances and availability of replacement items.


5. Lost or Stolen Equipment

You are responsible for the rented equipment's safekeeping during the rental period. If the equipment is lost or stolen, you will be charged the full replacement cost.


6. Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed using the same payment method you used for the initial booking, unless otherwise agreed upon.

7. Terms and Conditions:

  • Refunds are subject to the specific terms outlined in the rental agreement.

  • Security deposit refund is contingent upon adherence to the cancellation policy and equipment condition.


8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Returns Policy, please contact us via: Email: info@grandoccasionrental.ie Phone: 0851563498


9. Changes to This Returns Policy

We may update our Returns Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. The updated policy will be posted on our website, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly
